# Straunt – Visual Inspiration Vault

This document stores the functional layout architecture ("Archetypes") derived from high-performing reference images. 
**CRITICAL RULE**: While the layout geometry comes from the reference, the colors and typography MUST always map to the `Straunt Design System` (`#F97A34`, `#FFFFFF`, `#161616`, Lexend font).

---

## Archetype A: "The Punchy Framework" (Ref: Images 1 & 2)
**Use Case**: Educational Carousel / "How To" posts / Hiring tips. Best for step-by-step continuity.

### Visual Rules:
- **Background**: Primarily clean white (`#FFFFFF`).
- **Typography Frame**: Left-aligned or centered massive headlines (`Lexend Bold`).
- **Motif (The "Joiner")**: Use interlocking geometric shapes (half-circles, connecting lines) that span across the seam of two slides. Use Straunt Orange (`#F97A34`) or a light tint of it for these shapes.
- **Imagery**: Cutout photography (hands, objects) floating on white. No full-bleed photo backgrounds.
- **Data/Text**: Ensure black text on white backgrounds.

---

## Archetype B: "The Product Integration" (Ref: Image 3)
**Use Case**: Feature announcements / Workflow explanations / "Show don't tell" posts.

### Visual Rules:
- **Background**: Ambient, slightly darkened food/restaurant or warehouse photography.
- **Motif (The "Cut")**: Diagonal, angular cuts of solid color (White or Light Gray) slicing across the photo background to create a stage for text.
- **UI Elements**: "Mock UI" overlays simulating the product (chat bubbles, order tickets, option selectors) floating above the background with soft shadows.
- **Trust Elements**: Include Trustpilot icons or similar social proof on the first and last slides.

---

## Archetype C: "The Editorial Zine" (Ref: Images 4 & 5)
**Use Case**: Founder stories / Testimonials / Thought Leadership quotes. Highly humanizing.

### Visual Rules:
- **Background**: Subtle, light-mode engineering grid (`#F5F7FB` background with 15% opacity gray lines).
- **Sticky Notes**: Use "Sticky Note" floating card layouts. Give them a soft drop-shadow. The card background should be White.
- **Asymmetry**: Tilt photography and sticky notes by 2-5 degrees off-axis to create friction.
- **Accents**: Use thick, clean Straunt Orange marker lines to highlight specific words in the text block. Do not use random pastel colors (no purple/green). Strip out the "messiness" but keep the edgy placement.
