# Straunt – Value Propositions

This document outlines why restaurants should choose Straunt — the core USPs, key messaging pillars, and the problems it solves better than any alternative.

---

## Core Positioning Statement

> **Straunt is the only all-in-one AI Restaurant OS that unifies front-desk operations, delivery management, customer marketing, and 24/7 phone AI into a single platform — replacing 5-10 disconnected tools with one system that pays for itself.**

---

## The Problem Straunt Solves

Modern restaurants are forced to stitch together multiple tools — a POS, a delivery tablet, a phone, a marketing tool, a loyalty app, a reputation tool — each with its own login, its own data, and its own cost. This fragmentation causes:

- **Missed calls = missed revenue**: Every unanswered phone call is a lost order and a lost customer forever.
- **30% marketplace commission drain**: Uber Eats, DoorDash, and Grubhub take a massive cut of every order.
- **Operational chaos**: Staff juggling 3–4 tablets, manually entering orders, and managing multiple dashboards.
- **Marketing blind spots**: No time, no data, and no system to bring customers back.
- **Reputation gaps**: Negative reviews go live because there's no system to intercept them before they're posted.
