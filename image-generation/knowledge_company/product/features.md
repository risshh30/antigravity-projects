# Straunt – Product Features

Straunt is an all-in-one AI-powered restaurant management platform. It unifies POS, online ordering, phone answering, marketing, and operations into a single connected system designed to help restaurants acquire customers, operate efficiently, and grow revenue.

---

## 1. AI Agents

### AI Order Taking (AI Phone Ordering)
- **24/7 AI Phone Answering** – AI automatically answers every inbound call and takes orders without staff involvement.
- **After-Hours Coverage** – Captures late-night and overflow orders when staff are unavailable.
- **Missed Call Recovery** – Turns missed calls into confirmed orders via automated follow-up.
- **Menu & Question Handling** – Answers guest questions about the menu instantly and accurately.
- **Upsells Built In** – Automatically suggests add-ons and upsells during the call to increase ticket size by 10–20%.
- **Order Accuracy** – No misheard orders or re-rings; orders are confirmed before submission.
- **Direct POS Injection** – Completed orders are sent directly into the POS without any staff data entry.
- **Impact Metrics**: 15–30% lift in phone order volume; 40–60% reduction in missed calls during rush.

### AI Reservations
- Guests can book, change, or cancel tables by phone at any time without waiting.
- Handles common dining questions clearly and consistently.
- Hosts remain focused on in-house guests instead of phone management.

### AI Marketer
- Creates personalized text and email campaigns automatically.
- Drives repeat visits and off-peak demand using real customer behavior and ordering patterns.
- Keeps the restaurant top-of-mind without manual marketing effort.
- Sends targeted promotions that bring lapsed customers back.

### AI Operator
- **Order Monitoring** – Keeps every order on track.
- **Inventory Awareness** – Alerts staff about stock issues before they become a problem.
- **Customer Requests** – Handles updates, changes, and guest inquiries.
- **Issue Resolution** – Fixes problems before they escalate.
- **Daily Summaries** – Provides at-a-glance reports of daily restaurant activity.
- **Workflow Automation** – Reduces manual workload for the entire team.

### AI Demand Forecaster
- Predicts demand patterns to help with staffing and inventory preparation.

### AI Catering Outreach
- Automates outreach to potential catering clients.

## 2. Straunt POS (Point of Sale)
- Modern POS built specifically for AI-driven restaurants.
- Syncs orders, loyalty programs, and every sales channel in real time.
- Next-gen hardware and software designed for lightning-fast checkout.

---

## 3. Straunt Dash (Delivery Aggregator)
- **Order Consolidation** – All Uber Eats, DoorDash, Grubhub, and direct orders appear in one unified dashboard.
- **Menu & Price Management** – Update items, prices, photos, or availability once and push everywhere instantly.
- **Automatic POS Injection** – Every delivery order flows directly into the POS with items, modifiers, and notes intact.
- **Smart Kitchen Routing** – Orders are automatically split and routed to the correct station (grill, fryer, bar, prep).
- **Virtual Brands** – Launch delivery-only brands using existing kitchen and staff; all managed inside one system.
- **Impact Metrics**: 60–90% reduction in order errors; saves 5–10 hours per week; 20–35% faster ticket flow.
