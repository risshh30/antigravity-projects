# Straunt – Integrations

Straunt integrates with 100+ applications and platforms so restaurant operators don't have to log into each tool separately. Every integration is designed to feed data back into the Straunt platform, keeping operations unified.

---

## Delivery & Marketplace Platforms
These platforms are natively supported through **Straunt Dash** for consolidated order management and menu sync:

| Platform | Integration Type |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Uber Eats** | Order consolidation, menu sync, POS injection |
| **DoorDash** | Order consolidation, menu sync, POS injection |
| **Grubhub** | Order consolidation, menu sync, POS injection |

- Orders from all platforms flow directly into the POS with items, modifiers, and special instructions.
- Menu prices and availability can be updated once and pushed to all platforms simultaneously.
- Marketplace orders are displayed on one centralized dashboard.

---

## Communication & Ordering Channels
Straunt supports multi-channel customer communication and ordering through **Straunt Connect** and **Straunt Direct**:

| Channel | Capability |
|---------|-----------|
| **Phone / VoIP** | AI-powered call answering, order taking, and reservation management |
| **WhatsApp** | Direct ordering and automated guest messaging automation |
| **SMS / Text Messaging** | Business texting, campaign delivery, loyalty messaging |
| **Email** | Automated campaign delivery, loyalty communications |
| **QR Codes** | Table-side ordering via scannable QR code menus |
| **Web (Online Ordering)** | Commission-free branded website ordering |
| **Branded Mobile App** | Restaurant-branded app for loyalty and direct ordering |
| **Kiosk** | Self-service in-dining ordering |

---

## POS (Point of Sale) Systems
- Straunt has its own native **Straunt POS** system.
- All external order sources (delivery apps, phone orders, online orders) inject directly into the POS workflow.
- No manual re-entry required.

---

## Marketing & CRM Platforms
Through **Straunt Campaigns** and **Straunt Marketing**:

- Customer data from all ordering channels feeds into a centralized CRM.
- Campaign delivery via email and SMS is built into the platform natively.
- Social media automation is supported for ongoing brand visibility.
- AI-powered ad targeting capabilities for local visibility campaigns.

---

## Loyalty & Customer Engagement
Through **Straunt Loyalty**:

- Loyalty rewards sync across all ordering channels (phone, web, QR, app, kiosk, delivery).
- Centralized customer profiles aggregate purchase history regardless of order source.

---

## Reputation & Review Platforms
Through **Straunt Reviews**:

- Automated integration with major review platforms including **Google**.
- Guest sentiment is monitored and escalated/amplified accordingly.
- Works to improve visibility in local search results.

---

## AI & Voice Technology
Through **Straunt AI** and **Straunt Connect**:

- Voice AI system that integrates with inbound phone systems.
- AI handles reservations, order taking, and general queries autonomously.
- All AI interactions sync results back into POS and CRM.

---

## Kitchen Operations
Through **Straunt Dash**:

- **Smart Kitchen Routing** – Integrated with kitchen display systems (KDS) to route orders to the correct station (grill, fryer, bar, prep).
- Orders from all channels (delivery apps, phone, web) are routed appropriately.

---

## Staffing & Payroll
Through **Straunt Ops**:

- Employee scheduling integration.
- Payroll management.
- Hiring workflow management.

---

## Summary

> Straunt advertises **100+ integrations** with the restaurant tech ecosystem. The core integration philosophy is **centralization** — all tools feed into a single platform so operators have one dashboard to run their entire restaurant.

All integrations are designed to eliminate the need for restaurants to log into multiple separate applications.
