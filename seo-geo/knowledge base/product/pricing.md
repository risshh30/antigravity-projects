## Pricing Model Overview

Straunt operates as a **modular, all-in-one SaaS platform** for restaurants. Pricing is structured around:
- The specific **solutions/modules** a restaurant subscribes to.
- The **business type and scale** of the restaurant.
- Likely a **monthly subscription** model typical of restaurant tech SaaS platforms.

No flat pricing is listed publicly. Straunt encourages prospective customers to schedule a demo to receive a custom quote based on their restaurant's specific needs.

## Value vs. Cost Context

Straunt positions its pricing against the cost of:
- **30% marketplace commissions** (Uber Eats, DoorDash) that Straunt Direct eliminates.
- **Missed revenue from unanswered calls** — Straunt AI captures 40–60% more calls.
- **Labor costs** for front-desk staff that AI replaces.
- **Manual marketing effort** replaced by automated campaigns.

The implied ROI message is that Straunt pays for itself by recovering revenue that restaurants are currently losing.
