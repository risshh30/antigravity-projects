# Title and Headline Formulas

Proven title structures that drive clicks and rankings. All formulas target 50-60 characters for optimal SERP display. **Always run the Title Writing Checklist before finalising any title.**

---

## Formula Reference

### 1. Numbered List
**Pattern**: `[Number] [Adjective] [Topic] [Qualifier/Benefit]`
- "7 Ways to Improve Your SEO Rankings in 2024"
- "15 Content Marketing Strategies That Actually Work"
- "5 Quick Wins for Better Site Performance"

**Rules**:
- Odd numbers (7, 9, 15) outperform even numbers
- Use specific numbers — never "over 10"
- Keep total under 30 for credibility

---

### 2. How-To
| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| How to [Goal] in [Timeframe] | "How to Rank on Google in 30 Days" |
| How to [Goal] Without [Objection] | "How to Increase Traffic Without Paid Ads" |
| How to [Goal] (Even If [Limitation]) | "How to Rank #1 (Even If You're a New Website)" |
| How to [Goal] Like [Authority] | "How to Do Keyword Research Like an SEO Pro" |

---

### 3. Question
| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| What Is [Topic]? [Hook] | "What Is GEO? Everything You Need to Know" |
| Why [Action] [Result] | "Why Your Website Isn't Ranking" |
| Should You [Action]? [Quick Answer] | "Should You Buy Backlinks? (Spoiler: No)" |
| When Should You [Action] | "When Should You Update Your SEO Strategy" |

---

### 4. Comparison
| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|

| [A] or [B] for [Audience/Goal] | "Shopify or WooCommerce for Small Business SEO" |

### 5. Ultimate Guide
| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|

### 6. Power Words
**Pattern**: `[Power Word] [Topic] [Qualifier]`

| Category | Words | Example |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Authority | Proven, Tested, Verified, Certified | "Proven Strategies to Double Your Organic Traffic" |
| Urgency | Now, Today, Fast, Quick, Instantly | "Quick Wins: Improve SEO Performance Today" |
| Value | Free, Complete, Essential, Ultimate | "The Essential SEO Checklist for 2024" |
| Exclusivity | Secret, Hidden, Little-Known, Insider | "7 Little-Known SEO Tactics That Actually Work" |
| Emotional | Powerful, Effortless, Simple, Easy | "Simple SEO Strategies for Busy Entrepreneurs" |

## CTR Optimization

### Boost CTR With
- **Year/Timeframe**: "SEO Trends for 2024" (signals freshness)
- **Brackets**: "Email Marketing Tips (That Actually Work)"
- **Numbers**: "7 Ways...", "15 Tools..."
- **Power words**: Ultimate, Essential, Proven, Secret
- **Target audience**: "...for Beginners", "...for Small Businesses", "...for B2B"

### Kill CTR By Doing This
- Writing in ALL CAPS
- Excessive punctuation (!!!, ???)
- Vague generic titles ("SEO Tips", "Marketing Guide")
- Misleading or clickbait promises

## Title Writing Checklist
Run this before finalising every title:

- [ ] Primary keyword included naturally
- [ ] Length is 50-60 characters
- [ ] Matches search intent (informational / transactional / navigational)
- [ ] Contains a power word or emotional trigger
- [ ] Specific and concrete — not vague
- [ ] Promises clear value or benefit
- [ ] Accurate — does not mislead
- [ ] No clickbait
- [ ] Unique from competitor titles
- [ ] Still readable when truncated on mobile
